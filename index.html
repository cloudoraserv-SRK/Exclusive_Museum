The Sandalwood Story

Each Exclusive Museum watch is carved from natural sandalwood, a material treasured for centuries for its fragrance and beauty.

Over time the wood slowly releases its natural aroma, making every watch feel personal and alive.

Every watch is crafted after order by skilled artisans, and a professional video of the crafting process is delivered to the buyer.

Crafting Process

Wood Selection

Rare sandalwood blocks are selected for texture and aroma.

Hand Carving

Artisans carefully shape the watch case from a single wood piece.

Movement Assembly

Japanese quartz movement is installed for reliable precision.

Final Finishing

Every watch is polished, inspected and prepared for delivery.
