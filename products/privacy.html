Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, protect, and retain customer information across orders, support, and delivery updates.

Applies to all Exclusive Museum storefront and order interactions.
For data requests, contact `sales@exclusivemuseum.cloud`.
Last reviewed: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during checkout, order tracking, support conversations, and account or admin-related contact.

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Shipping address, city, country, and postal code.
  • Order reference details and communication history.
  • Basic technical information such as device or IP-related fraud-prevention data when available.

2. How We Use Information

  • To confirm, process, and fulfill orders.
  • To provide payment review, crafting, and shipping updates.
  • To respond to support, warranty, or return questions.
  • To improve storefront performance, fraud checks, and service quality.

3. Payment Data

Payment instructions may be completed through manual review or third-party services depending on the method selected. We only retain the reference information necessary to verify a transaction.

We do not intentionally store full card credentials on our own storefront pages.

4. Sharing With Partners

We may share limited information with logistics providers, payment facilitators, analytics vendors, and support tools strictly to operate the business and complete your order.

5. Data Retention

Order records, fulfillment notes, and service history may be retained for accounting, warranty, fraud review, and customer support purposes for a commercially reasonable period.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of personal information where legally permitted and operationally practical.

7. Updates

We may revise this policy as the storefront, payment flows, or fulfillment process evolves. The latest version will always appear on this page.
