Shipping & Returns

1. Handcrafted Production

All Exclusive Museum watches are individually handcrafted after an order is placed.

Crafting Time: 30 – 45 Days
Each piece is produced specifically for the buyer.

2. Shipping & Delivery

Once crafting is completed, the product will be shipped through trusted international courier partners.

  • Worldwide shipping available
  • Tracking number provided after shipment
  • Delivery time depends on destination country

3. Order Tracking

Customers will receive a tracking ID via email once the product is shipped. Orders can be tracked using the tracking page on our website.

4. Shipping Charges

  • Free worldwide shipping on all orders
  • No hidden handling charges

5. Returns Policy

Because each product is made to order and handcrafted, returns are only accepted in case of manufacturing defects or shipping damage.

  • Return request must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery
  • Product must remain unused
  • Original packaging must be preserved

6. Refund Policy

If a return is approved due to a verified defect, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

7. Order Cancellation

Orders may only be cancelled within 12 hours of purchase. After production begins, cancellations may not be possible.
